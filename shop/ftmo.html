FTMO Challenge: 1-Step (2026 Update)

FTMO introduced a new 1-Step evaluation that removes the separate Verification phase, so you complete a single evaluation stage. The published structure includes a 10% Profit Target with no time limits, a 90% reward, and a 50% Best Day Rule focused on consistency rather than “one lucky day”.

Key 1-Step rules (high level):
• Single evaluation phase (no separate Verification).
• 10% Profit Target, no time limits.
• Maximum Daily Loss: 3% of the initial simulated balance (recalculated each day).
• Maximum Loss: 10% using End-of-Day (EOD) trailing updates (updates once per day).
• Best Day Rule: 50% (not treated as a breach; you can keep trading to “dilute” it).

If you want an efficient path, 1-Step is built for traders who already have a stable process and want fewer phases. If you trade higher frequency or aim to “rush” targets, the consistency rules (like Best Day 50%) can influence how you pace your trading.

FTMO FAQ (Updated)

What is FTMO 1-Step?

FTMO 1-Step is a single-phase evaluation with a 10% Profit Target and no time limits, with risk objectives including a 3% Maximum Daily Loss and a 10% Maximum Loss using an End-of-Day trailing model, plus a 50% Best Day Rule.

What is the Best Day Rule (50%)?

Best Day Rule checks if your most profitable day is more than 50% of your total profit. If it is, you are not failed—you can continue trading until the ratio drops below 50%.
