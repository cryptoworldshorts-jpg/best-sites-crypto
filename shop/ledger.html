Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is Ledger Nano X safe?

Yes. Ledger Nano X is one of the safest hardware wallets in the world, protected by a certified secure element chip and offline key storage. Even if your computer or smartphone is hacked, your crypto remains safe.

What cryptocurrencies can I store?

You can store over 5,000 assets including Bitcoin, Ethereum, BNB, XRP, Solana, Dogecoin, Polygon, Cardano, and thousands of ERC-20 tokens.

What happens if I lose the device?

Your crypto is not stored on the device — only your private keys. With your 24-word recovery phrase, you can restore your wallet on any Ledger device.

Is Ledger Nano X better than Nano S Plus?

Nano X includes Bluetooth, a larger screen, and more internal storage for apps. It's designed for users who want mobility and premium features.
