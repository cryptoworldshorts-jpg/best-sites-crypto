What Is Binance?

Launched in 2017 by Changpeng Zhao (CZ), Binance has quickly grown from a small exchange to a global crypto giant with more than 150 million registered users in 2025. With its powerful trading tools, incredibly deep liquidity, wide asset selection, and very low fees, Binance remains the number one choice for traders, investors, and institutions worldwide.
