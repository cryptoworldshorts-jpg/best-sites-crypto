3. Help! My Deposit Hasn't Arrived

Blockchain transactions are usually fast, but delays happen. If your deposit is missing on KCEX, follow this checklist before contacting support:

  • Check the Network: Did you send USDT via TRC20 (Tron) but selected ERC20 (Ethereum) on the receiving address? This is the #1 error. Always match the networks perfectly.
  • Check Confirmations: Copy your Transaction ID (TXID) and paste it into a blockchain explorer (like TronScan or Etherscan). If the status is "Confirming," KCEX cannot credit you yet. You must wait for the blockchain block confirmations.
  • Minimum Deposit: Did you send less than the minimum? (e.g., sending 2 USDT when the minimum is 5 USDT). If so, the system ignores it until you send more to reach the threshold.
